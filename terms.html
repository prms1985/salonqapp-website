About SalonQ & ZW Technologies

ZW Technologies Private Limited ("Company", "we", "us", "our") is a technology company incorporated under the Companies Act, 2013, registered in Tamil Nadu, India. We operate the SalonQ platform — India's first AI-powered salon queue management system.

SalonQ is accessible through:

  • The SalonQ Business application for salon owners and staff

Queue Joining

  • When you join a queue on SalonQ, you are making a commitment to attend the salon at the estimated time.
  • You must arrive at the salon within 5 minutes of receiving your "Your Turn Now" notification.
  • Repeated no-shows may result in account restrictions at the discretion of the Salon Partner.
  • You may cancel your queue position at any time without penalty through the app.

QR Code Usage

  • QR codes are valid only at the specific salon that issued them.

Notifications

  • SalonQ is not responsible for missed turns due to notification settings on your device, particularly on Chinese OEM Android devices that may restrict background app activity.

Customer Conduct

  • You must treat salon staff and other customers with respect.

  • Ratings and reviews must be honest and not defamatory.

Payment at Salon: SalonQ does not process customer payments for salon services. All payments for haircuts, beauty services, etc. are made directly at the salon by whatever method the salon accepts (cash, UPI, card). SalonQ only processes subscription payments from salon owners.
