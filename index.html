FAQ

Common questions

Do customers need to download the app to join a queue?
+
Customers can scan the QR code at the salon entrance to join the queue without downloading the app. For full features like live tracking, ETA alerts, and remote joining, they can download the SalonQ customer app from Google Play or App Store.
Why is SalonQ cheaper than WhatsApp Business notifications?
+
SalonQ uses Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) for all customer notifications — completely free at ₹0 per message. WhatsApp Business API charges ₹0.60–₹1.00 per message. A busy salon sending 300 notifications per day saves ₹5,400–₹9,000 per month — more than 10× the SalonQ subscription cost.
How accurate is the AI wait time prediction?
+
Our AI uses a weighted moving average of the last 30 service logs for each staff member and service type. Accuracy starts at around 60% for a new salon and grows to 94%+ as more data accumulates. We always show a confidence percentage so customers know how reliable the estimate is.
What happens if a customer misses their notification?
+
SalonQ has a 6-step escalation system. If the FCM push is not acknowledged within 3 minutes, an in-app banner appears the next time the customer opens the app. After 5 minutes, an SMS fallback is sent. We also specifically handle Chinese Android phones (Xiaomi, OPPO, Vivo) that block background apps with a guided battery optimisation setup at onboarding.
Can I manage multiple salon branches?
+
Multi-branch support is coming soon on the Boss plan. Currently each salon is managed as a separate entity. If you run a chain, contact us at parameswaran@salonqapp.in for enterprise pricing.
Is my data safe? Where is it stored?
+
All customer data is stored on servers in Mumbai, India (AWS ap-south-1 region) in compliance with India's DPDPA 2023. We use HTTPS encryption in transit, encrypted storage at rest, JWT authentication, and role-based access control. We never sell your data. Read our full Privacy Policy for details.
How do I get started as a salon owner?
+
Email parameswaran@salonqapp.in or download the SalonQ Business app and register your salon. Our team personally onboards you — we set up the tablet at your reception, train your staff, and stay until your first 10 customers have been successfully processed through SalonQ. All included in your subscription.
