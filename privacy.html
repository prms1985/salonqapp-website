By using the SalonQ platform, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of our services.

How We Use Your Information

We use the personal data we collect for the following specific purposes:

  • Account creation and authentication: Your phone number is used to create and verify your SalonQ account via OTP.
  • Queue management: Your name and token number are shared with the salon to manage your place in the queue.
  • AI-powered ETA predictions: Service history and staff performance data are used to calculate accurate wait time estimates.
  • Push notifications: Your FCM device token is used to send you free queue status alerts, ETA notifications, and service completion messages.
  • Map and location services: Your GPS coordinates are used to show nearby salons with live wait times on the map. We do not track your location in the background.
  • Billing and invoicing: Salon owner business details are used to generate GST-compliant invoices for subscription payments.
  • Customer support: Contact details are used to resolve complaints and provide assistance.
  • Platform safety: We use data to detect fraud, prevent abuse, and maintain platform security.
  • Analytics and improvement: Anonymised and aggregated usage data helps us improve the platform.

We do NOT use your data for: Targeted advertising, selling to third-party marketers, profiling for purposes unrelated to SalonQ, or any purpose not listed above.

Data Storage & Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data from unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure:

  • Server location: All customer data is stored on servers in Mumbai, India (AWS ap-south-1 region) ensuring data localisation compliance with Indian laws.
  • Encryption in transit: All data transmitted between the app and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS 1.3.
  • Encryption at rest: Database storage is encrypted at rest on Neon.tech (AWS infrastructure).
  • Authentication: JWT-based authentication with 15-minute access tokens and 30-day refresh tokens. OTP-only login — no passwords stored.
  • Access control: Role-based access — customer data is only accessible to authorised staff of the relevant salon and our platform administrators.
  • Audit logging: All administrative actions are logged in an immutable audit trail.
  • Payment security: Card and payment data is handled exclusively by Razorpay (PCI DSS Level 1 certified). We never store full card numbers.

Security Incident: In the unlikely event of a data breach that may harm you, we will notify you and relevant authorities within 72 hours as required by applicable law.

Your Rights Under DPDPA 2023

Under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023, you have the following rights as a Data Principal:

  • Right to Access (Section 11 DPDPA): You can request a summary of what personal data we hold about you and how it is being processed.
  • Right to Correction and Erasure (Section 12 DPDPA): You can request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data, and request deletion of data that is no longer necessary.
  • Right to Grievance Redressal (Section 13 DPDPA): You have the right to have your grievances addressed by our Grievance Officer within the timelines prescribed by law.
  • Right to Nominate (Section 14 DPDPA): You may nominate another individual to exercise your rights in the event of your death or incapacity.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: You can withdraw your consent to data processing at any time. This will not affect the lawfulness of processing before withdrawal. Withdrawing consent may limit your ability to use SalonQ services.

How to Exercise Your Rights: Email parameswaran@salonqapp.in with the subject line "Data Rights Request — [Your Name]". We will respond within 30 days as required under DPDPA 2023. For account deletion, you can also use the Delete Account option within the SalonQ app settings.

Push Notifications & FCM

SalonQ uses Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) by Google to send push notifications to your device. This is how we alert you when your turn is approaching, when your service is complete, and for other queue updates.

  • What we send: Queue position updates, ETA alerts, service completion notices, and booking confirmations.
  • Cost to you: Push notifications via FCM are completely free. We do not use WhatsApp Business API (which charges per message) for standard notifications.
  • SMS fallback: If push notifications are not acknowledged within 5 minutes (e.g., device is offline), we may send an SMS via MSG91 as a fallback for critical alerts. SMS is charged at ₹0.20 per message to us — not to you.
  • How to disable: You can disable notifications at any time through your phone's notification settings or through the SalonQ app settings screen.
  • FCM and Google: Firebase FCM is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. Device tokens processed by FCM are subject to Google's data practices in addition to ours.
